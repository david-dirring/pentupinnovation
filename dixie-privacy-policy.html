Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 11, 2026

Dixie's Right Hand Paw is operated by Pentup Innovation LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our SMS messaging service.

Information We Collect

When you opt in to receive text messages from Dixie's Right Hand Paw, we collect your name and mobile phone number as provided through our SMS consent form. We may also collect information related to your pet grooming appointments, including pet names, appointment dates, and service details, as needed to provide appointment confirmations, reminders, and customer support.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information solely to send appointment confirmations, reminders, and scheduling updates; respond to customer support inquiries via SMS; and communicate changes to your scheduled appointments.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your phone number or personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share your information only with service providers who help us deliver SMS messages (such as our messaging platform provider), and only to the extent necessary to operate this service.

Data Retention

We retain your phone number and consent records for as long as you remain opted in to our messaging service. If you opt out by replying STOP, we will retain a record of your opt-out request to ensure we honor it, but will no longer send you messages.

Your Choices

You may opt out of receiving text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. You may also reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us directly at david@pentupinnovation.com.

Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via text message or by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised effective date.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 16, 2026

Pentup Innovation LLC ("we," "us," or "our") provides AI-powered customer communication tools for small businesses. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our SMS messaging platform.

Information We Collect

When you opt in to receive text messages through our platform, or initiate a text conversation with a Pentup Innovation-powered phone number, we may collect:

  • Your name and mobile phone number as provided through our consent form or as identified by your incoming text message
  • The content of text messages you send to and receive from our platform
  • Information related to your appointments or service interactions with our client businesses, such as appointment dates, times, and service details
  • Opt-in and opt-out records, including timestamps and method of consent

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information solely to:

  • Send appointment confirmations, reminders, and scheduling updates on behalf of our client businesses
  • Respond to customer support inquiries you initiate via SMS
  • Communicate changes to your scheduled appointments
  • Maintain records of your consent and opt-out preferences
  • Improve the quality and reliability of our messaging platform

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your phone number or personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share your information only with:

  • The specific client business you are communicating with, to the extent necessary to manage your appointments and service interactions
  • Service providers who help us deliver SMS messages (such as our telecommunications provider), solely to the extent necessary to operate this service
  • As required by law, regulation, or legal process

Data Retention

We retain your phone number, message history, and consent records for as long as you remain opted in to our messaging service. If you opt out by replying STOP, we will retain a record of your opt-out request to ensure we honor it, but will no longer send you messages. We may retain anonymized or aggregated data for analytics purposes.

Your Choices

You may opt out of receiving text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. You may also reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us directly at support@pentupinnovation.com.

Security

We implement reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include encrypted data transmission, access controls, and secure storage of consent records.

Children's Privacy

Our SMS messaging services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via text message or by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised effective date.
